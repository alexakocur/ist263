PRIMER!!
This is such an important part of your makeup routine. Primer is something that is crucial! Primer is used so that your makeup stays on your face all day.
FOUNDATION OR SKIN TINT
Foundation or skin tint is what you call the “base” of your face. After you use the primer you put on your base. It is a skin colored liquid that you put all over your face. Foundation is a more high coverage while skin tint has a lighter coverage. Foundation and skin tint is mainly used to even out your skin tone, hide and blemishes and provide coverage. Personally, I use skintint because I don't love how heavy foundation feels on my face.
CONCEALER
Concealer is the next step. After your base (foundation and or skintint) you put on concealer. Concealer is similar to foundation except it's much thicker. I generally use concealer that is a lighter shade so that it brightens up my face. Concealer also helps alot with adding more coverage over stubborn blemishes and imperfections.
COUNTOUR/BRONZER
Contour is used to sculpt and add dimension to the face. It essentially carves out your face. Don't worry I know this sounds weird, but it really does work.
BLUSH
Blush is my all time favorite makeup product. I love how you can use any color blush you want pink, red, purple, it's so versatile. Blush is what adds a color to your face, it gives you the “rosy” cheek effect.
HIGHLIGHTER
Highlighter is a product that has a lot of benefits. Not only does it add shimmer to your face to make you look super bright, but it could also be used to lift your cheekbone.
